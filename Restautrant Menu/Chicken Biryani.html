Chicken Biryani

Recipe Summary:

A classic dish made with marinated chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of spices.

Recipe Description:

Marinated chicken cooked with fragrant basmati rice and a mix of spices, resulting in a flavorful and aromatic dish.
The chicken is marinated in a blend of spices, yogurt, and lemon juice, then cooked with basmati rice and a hint of saffron.
Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a dollop of raita (yogurt and cucumber sauce).
