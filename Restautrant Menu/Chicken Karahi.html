Chicken Karahi

Recipe Summary: A classic Pakistani dish, Chicken Karahi is a spicy and flavorful curry made with marinated chicken, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and fresh chilies.

Recipe Description:

Tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich and aromatic curry sauce with a perfect balance of spices, tangy tomatoes, and fresh herbs.
Serve with naan or rice for a delicious meal.
