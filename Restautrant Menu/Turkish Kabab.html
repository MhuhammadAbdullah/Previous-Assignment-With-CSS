Turkish Kabab

Recipe Summary: Skewers of marinated lamb, beef, and vegetables grilled to perfection, served with a side of tangy Turkish sauce.

Recipe Description:

Skewers of marinated lamb, beef, and vegetables grilled to perfection.
The meat is tender and flavorful, with a slight char on the outside. Serve with a side of tangy Turkish sauce, rice pilaf, and crusty bread for a delicious and authentic Turkish meal.
